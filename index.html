Who We Are - 01

Built on a name.
Driven by a legacy.

The name Suncerray belongs to two women. A mother and a sister. Both gone. The mother didn't always make the right choices - but she made sure her children never felt what she lacked. She gave everything she had with the limited resources available to her. The sister carried the name forward.

Suncerray Capital Inc. was founded on that legacy - to move resources to builders who figure it out with what they have. That resourcefulness deserves capital behind it. That vision deserves a real chance.

"She made sure her children never felt what she lacked."

Suncerray Capital is not a traditional fund. It is a capital vehicle built with purpose - to move the right resources to the right builders at the right time. The portfolio comes first. The mission extends as it grows.

What We Back - 02

Portfolio first.
Mission extends from there.

Suncerray Capital's current mandate is funding the growth of the Young's Multimedia Holdings portfolio through the path to profitability. Seven subsidiaries. Six verticals. 34 software products across YDA.

As the portfolio matures, it is our intention to extend Suncerray Capital's reach beyond YMMH - selectively backing early-stage founders where the opportunity, the mission, and the people align. That lane is being built deliberately, not prematurely.
